Sequence Exit Rules
Stops on Email Reply.

The sequence will stop if the contact replies to any email on the sequence or forwards your email to someone who replies to you.

Stops on Any Email Communication

The sequence will stop if you and your contact have any email communication outside of the sequence.

Stops on Any Text Messages

The sequence will stop if you and your contact exchange text messages outside of the sequence.

Stops if Connected

The sequence will stop if you check off a manual call task confirming you connected with the contact.
